What Is Lactate Threshold?

The exercise intensity where lactate production exceeds clearance, causing accumulation.

LT1 = aerobic threshold (sustainable for hours)
LT2 = anaerobic threshold (sustainable ~1 hour)

Training at/around these thresholds maximizes adaptations for endurance athletes and longevity seekers alike.
